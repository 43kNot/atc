Dolphin

There are currently 42 species of dolphins.
The orca (killer whale) is the largest dolphin.

  • Scientific Name: Delphinidae
  • Average Length: 5 to 13 feet
  • Average Lifespan: 60 Years
  • Habitat: Seas,oceans and in some rivers

Dolphins are socially skilled, intelligent, agile and playful creatures that share many emotional similarities with humans and are known for their array of sophisticated vocalizations such as clicks, whistles and squeals which they use for their well-developed communication and echolocation skills.
