Dolphin

There are currently 42 species of dolphins.
The orca is the largest dolphin.

  • Scientific Name: Delphinidae
  • Average Length: 5 to 13 feet
  • Average Lifespan: 60 Years
  • Habitat: Seas,oceans and in some rivers

Dolphins are socially skilled, intelligent and playful creatures that share many emotional similarities with humans and are known for their array of sophisticated echolocation vocalizations - clicks, whistles and squeals, which they use to communicate & navigate.
